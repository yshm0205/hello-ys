## ðŸ“ Detailed Prompts

### 1. Generate Terms of Service (Korean)

```
You are an IT specialist lawyer in Korea. Write **Terms of Service** for "[ServiceName]" service.

## Service Info
- Service Name: [ServiceName]
- Service Type: [e.g., SaaS subscription service]
- Operator: [Company/Individual]
- Email: [Email]

## Applicable Laws
- Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce (cancellation rights, etc.)
- Act on Regulation of Terms and Conditions

## Key Content
1. Service agreement formation
2. Subscription cancellation and refund policy (reflecting legal requirements)
3. Company liability disclaimer (excluding intent/gross negligence)
4. Copyright and usage license
5. Jurisdiction: Seoul Central District Court

Output in markdown format.
```

### 2. Generate Terms of Service (English)

```
Act as an international IT lawyer. Write a **Terms of Service** for a SaaS product called "[Service Name]".

## Service Info
- Service Name: [Service Name]
- Company: [Company Name]
- Email: [Email]
- Jurisdiction: [e.g., Republic of Korea]

## Key Clauses
1. Subscription & Cancellation policy
2. Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)
3. Limitation of Liability (standard "As Is" clauses)
4. Governing Law: Republic of Korea
5. Dispute Resolution: Seoul Central District Court

Output in markdown format.
```

---

### 3. Generate Privacy Policy (Korean)

```
You are a Korean Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA) expert. Write a **Privacy Policy**.

## Service Info
- Service Name: [ServiceName]
- Privacy Officer: [Name]
- Email: [Email]

## External Services Used (Including International Transfer)
- Supabase (USA, Database)
- LemonSqueezy (USA, Payment Processing)
- Resend (USA, Email Delivery)

## Required Disclosures (Legal Obligations)
1. Purpose and retention period of personal information processing
2. Third-party provision status
3. **International Transfer**: Include overseas server delegation (country, timing, method)
4. How data subjects can exercise their rights
5. Destruction procedures and methods
6. Privacy Officer (CPO) contact information

Output in markdown format.
```

### 4. Generate Privacy Policy (English - GDPR)

```
Act as a GDPR and CCPA privacy expert. Write a **Privacy Policy** for "[Service Name]".

## Service Info
- Data Controller: [Company Name]
- Contact: [Email]

## Processors (Third Parties)
- Supabase (Database, USA)
- LemonSqueezy (Payments, USA)
- Resend (Email, USA)

## Key Sections (GDPR Compliant)
1. Types of data collected (PII, Usage Data)
2. Purpose of processing
3. Data Retention policy
4. International Data Transfers (Standard Contractual Clauses)
5. User Rights (Access, Deletion, Portability - GDPR Art. 15-20)
6. Cookie Policy reference
7. Contact information

Output in markdown format.
```
